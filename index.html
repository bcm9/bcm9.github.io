Benjamin Caswell-Midwinter

Researcher in London, UK | Health and Wellbeing Research

About Me

. My ultimate goal is to support health and wellbeing through evidence-based innovation. I'm particularly interested in:
  • Conducting clinical, operational, and economic evaluations of new medical devices and health technologies, ensuring a balanced approach to innovation.
  • Advancing the understanding and treatment of hearing loss, focusing on the personalisation of hearing devices to meet individual benefit and satisfaction.
  • Leveraging technology in clinical settings to streamline processes, freeing up time for face-to-face behavioural treatments.
  • Programming to drive statistical insights, develop machine learning solutions, communicate data through visualizations, and automate tasks.
  • Machine learning diagnostics, natural language processing for qualitative analysis, and health economic modelling to inform decision-making.
My experience spans laboratory research, literature reviews, clinical trials, observational studies, qualitative research, and economic assessments, facilitating a holistic approach to innovation in health.
