Research and Analytics; Psychophysics / Physiological / Behavioural / Big Data

Hello, I'm Ben. I'm currently a data analyst in London, UK.

My background is in conducting psychophysical, physiological and data-driven research in auditory science.

The most common way to personalise hearing devices is to adjust the settings to the feedback of the user. The test signal in most cases is a very complex one - live voice. My PhD used psychophysics to work out what gain adjustments are noticeable - and meaningful - to those who most commonly use hearing devices; patients with hearing loss.

During my studies, I got really into programming.
